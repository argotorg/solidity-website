## The Survey Covers:

- Demographics and developer profiles
- Tooling and usage
- Developer experience
- Core Solidity
- Use of AI

This is the start of a broader, ongoing feedback program with the community. Your voice matters.

You can find the results of the previous Solidity Developer Survey [here](https://soliditylang.org/blog/2025/04/25/solidity-developer-survey-2024-results/). In 2024, a total of 1,342 developers from 89 different countries participated in the survey.

## Why Participate?

Your answers will directly help shape the future of the Solidity language, compiler, and surrounding tooling.

As a thank you, you'll have a chance to win a [Devcon 8](https://devcon.org/en/) ticket (just add your contact details at the end of the survey to enter the raffle).
